How to play?

Connect Four Game Instructions

  1. Objective: Your goal is to connect four of your pieces in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent does.
  2. Setup: The game is played on a grid with six rows and seven columns.
  3. Movement:
    1. Use the left and right arrow keys to move the falling piece to the desired column.
    2. Press the down arrow key to drop the piece into the selected column.
  4. Gameplay:
    1. Pieces fall from the top of the column and automatically drop to the lowest available spot.
    2. Each player takes turns to drop their colored pieces into the columns.
  5. Winning: The first player to connect four of their pieces in a row wins the game.
  6. Enjoy playing!
